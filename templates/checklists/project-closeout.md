# Project Closeout Checklist

Complete all items before moving a project to Archive status.

## Deliverables
- [ ] All deliverables received from vendor
- [ ] All deliverables accepted by government
- [ ] Final deliverable acceptance documented

## Financial
- [ ] Final invoice received from vendor
- [ ] Final invoice validated and processed
- [ ] Final ONI invoice submitted to ACC-RI
- [ ] Final invoice confirmed closed by ACC-RI
- [ ] No outstanding billing discrepancies

## Contract
- [ ] Contract status confirmed complete/closed
- [ ] All modifications closed or cancelled
- [ ] No pending contract actions

## Records Archive
- [ ] Final challenge text archived
- [ ] LOEs archived
- [ ] All vendor proposals (original + updates) archived
- [ ] Award documents archived
- [ ] All modifications archived
- [ ] All invoices archived
- [ ] Key email chains preserved
- [ ] Meeting notes and action trackers archived

## Lessons Learned
- [ ] Lessons learned captured while project is still fresh
- [ ] Key decisions documented with rationale:
  - Why challenge was drafted this way
  - Why vendor was selected
  - Why SOW changed
  - Why invoices were approved/rejected
- [ ] Recommendations for future similar projects noted

## Notifications
- [ ] Vendor notified of closeout
- [ ] Government sponsor notified
- [ ] ACC-RI notified
- [ ] Internal status tracker updated to "Archived"
