# Requesting an Updated Proposal from a Vendor

## Overview
After a vendor is selected, ONI requests an updated proposal aligned to the government's desired scope, period of performance, and funding. This is a critical step in vendor coordination.

**Related pipeline stage:** [Vendor Coordination](/docs/challenge-pipeline/05-vendor-coordination)

## Email Template

<span class="badge badge-oni">ONI</span> → <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span>

**Subject:** Updated Proposal Request – [Challenge Name]

**To:** <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span>

```
Good afternoon [Vendor POC Name],

We received interest from [Government Customer] in moving forward with a prototype
effort related to the [Challenge Name] challenge posted on the Colosseum Marketplace.

To support next steps under the ONI OTA, please provide an updated proposal
(original attached) that is aligned to the PoP, Funding, and scope below.

Period of Performance: [X months]
Funding: NTE $[amount]

Requested Scope:
1. [Scope item 1 with deliverable and target date]
2. [Scope item 2 with deliverable and target date]
3. [Scope item 3 with deliverable and target date]
...

Please let me know if you have any questions.
```

## Key Points
- Always attach the vendor's original proposal for reference
- Be specific about PoP, funding ceiling, and scope items
- Include target dates for major deliverables where known
- This request often follows a government down-select and SOW development
- The vendor's updated proposal will be reviewed against the government SOW for alignment
