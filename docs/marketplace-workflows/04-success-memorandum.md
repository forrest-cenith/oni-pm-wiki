# Success Memorandum

## Overview
A success memorandum captures the outcomes of an awarded effort — what the vendor achieved, the operational impact, and evidence supporting the value of the prototype work. This is used for internal reporting and stakeholder communication.

**Related pipeline stage:** [Archive](/docs/challenge-pipeline/08-archive)

## Steps

1. **Copy the Success Memorandum Template** into the relevant Ops folder for the project

2. **Open the contract that was awarded to the effort**
   - Located in the `1. Award Docs` subfolder

3. **Reach out to the vendor** to get their description of how the effort was successful
   - Ask them to describe outcomes, milestones hit, and mission impact in their own words

4. **Add the vendor's explanation** to the Success Memorandum template

5. **Gather supporting materials**
   - Original contract (helps contextualize the scope)
   - Kickoff charts that explain the scope in more detail
   - Any deliverable summaries or test results

## Tips
- Don't wait until the very end — start collecting success evidence during execution
- The vendor's own words are valuable — let them describe impact in their voice
- Include both quantitative outcomes (metrics, milestones) and qualitative impact (mission relevance, operational improvement)
- A strong success memo helps justify future OTA efforts to government stakeholders
