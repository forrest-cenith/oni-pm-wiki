# Requesting Reps & Certs from a Vendor

## Overview
Before award, vendors must provide Representations & Certifications and a current W-9. This can be requested proactively to streamline the award process.

**Related pipeline stage:** [Vendor Coordination](/docs/challenge-pipeline/05-vendor-coordination)

## Email Template

<span class="badge badge-oni">ONI</span> → <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span>

**Subject:** Pre-Award Documentation Request – [Challenge Name]

**To:** <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span>

```
Good afternoon [Vendor POC Name],

As we continue coordination with the Government on the [Challenge Name],
we would like to ensure we are prepared to move quickly should your team
be selected for award under the ONI OTA.

At your convenience, please complete and return the following:
- Attached Reps & Certs form
- Updated W9

Providing these in advance will help streamline the award process and
avoid delays once a selection decision is finalized.

Please let me know if you have any questions or need any additional information.
```

## Attachments to Include
- ONI OTA Agreement Holder Representations (fillable PDF)
- W-9 form (PDF)

## Key Points
- This can be sent proactively before final selection to avoid delays
- Both documents are standard — vendors should be familiar with them
- Track receipt in the [Post-Selection Vendor Package](/templates/checklists/post-selection-vendor-package) checklist
- Missing admin documents are a common blocker in vendor coordination
