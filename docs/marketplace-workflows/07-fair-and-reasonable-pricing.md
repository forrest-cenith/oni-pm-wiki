# Requesting Fair & Reasonable Pricing Clarification

## Overview
As part of the OTA award process, ONI must document a Fair & Reasonable price determination. This involves requesting a lightweight cost breakdown from the selected vendor.

**Related pipeline stage:** [Vendor Coordination](/docs/challenge-pipeline/05-vendor-coordination)

## Email Template

<span class="badge badge-oni">ONI</span> → <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span>

**Subject:** Fair & Reasonable Pricing Support – [Challenge Name]

**To:** <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span>

```
[Vendor Team / POC Name],

Congratulations — your proposed solution has been selected by the Government
customer for the [Challenge Name] opportunity. We appreciate your engagement
and the strength of your submission.

As part of the award process under OTA, ONI is required to document a Fair &
Reasonable price determination. To support this, we are requesting a lightweight
cost breakdown that explains how your proposed price was developed.

Please provide the following (no formal proposal required):

1. Labor Breakdown (LCATs)
   - Labor categories used (e.g., Engineer II, Program Manager, Technician)
   - Estimated hours per category
   - Fully burdened hourly rates (or explanation of rate basis)

2. Materials / ODCs
   - Major material components or subcontractors
   - Estimated costs and basis of estimate (vendor quotes, historical costs, etc.)

3. Other Cost Drivers
   - Any significant cost elements (e.g., testing, integration, facilities, travel)

4. Summary
   - Short narrative (1-2 paragraphs) explaining key assumptions and cost drivers

Format: A simple Excel sheet and/or brief narrative is sufficient — no need for
a formal cost proposal or certified pricing data.

Level of Detail: This is for transparency and rationale (not audit-level detail).
High-level but traceable estimates are appropriate.

Please submit at your earliest convenience to support timely award processing.
Let me know if you have any questions as you prepare this.
```

## Key Points
- This is not a formal cost proposal — keep the ask lightweight
- An Excel sheet + short narrative is sufficient
- The goal is transparency and traceability, not audit-level precision
- This supports ONI's Fair & Reasonable determination for OTA awards
