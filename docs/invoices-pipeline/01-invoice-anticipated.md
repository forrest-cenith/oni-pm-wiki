# Invoice Anticipated

> **ONI's invoicing model:** The subcontractor/vendor submits an invoice to ONI, and ONI generates the invoice to submit to ACC Rock Island, mapped to CLINs.

## Overview

ONI expects a vendor invoice based on milestone completion, periodic billing cadence, or deliverable acceptance. This stage is about getting ahead of the billing event so that when the invoice arrives, ONI is ready to validate, map, and submit without delay.

## Key Tasks / Checklist

- [ ] Review contract and milestone schedule to identify upcoming billing events
- [ ] Confirm vendor knows billing requirements, format expectations, and required backup documentation
- [ ] Confirm milestone acceptance evidence exists or is in progress
- [ ] Pre-check CLIN mapping and expected invoice amounts against the contract/billing worksheet
- [ ] Verify the correct billing entity, contract number, and mod version the vendor should reference
- [ ] Flag any upcoming billing events where acceptance or documentation gaps remain

## Documents Needed or Produced

| Document | Direction | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Contract / CLIN Structure | Input | Defines allowable billing line items and amounts |
| Milestone Acceptance Evidence | Input | Proof that the deliverable or milestone has been accepted by the government or ONI |
| Anticipated Invoice Tracker | Output | Internal log of expected billing events, amounts, and readiness status |

## Common Hangups & Resolutions

| Hangup | Resolution |
|---|---|
| Vendor bills before milestone acceptance is documented | Proactively confirm acceptance status with the government or ONI lead before the vendor's planned submission date |
| Billing amount does not match CLIN structure or expectations | Share the CLIN matrix and expected amounts with the vendor ahead of time so discrepancies are caught before submission |
| Missing backup documentation anticipated at invoice time | Identify required backup early and request it from the vendor or government acceptor before the billing event |

## Stakeholders & Roles

| Stakeholder | Role |
|---|---|
| Vendor Finance / PM | Prepares and submits invoice with required backup |
| ONI PM | Coordinates timing, confirms milestone status, pre-checks CLIN alignment |
| ONI Finance / Admin | Maintains anticipated invoice tracker, confirms billing expectations |
| Government Acceptor | Provides milestone or deliverable acceptance if required before billing |

## Typical Timeline

Should be triggered just before a planned billing event -- typically one to two weeks ahead of an expected invoice submission date. The goal is to surface and resolve issues before the invoice arrives.

## Tips & Lessons Learned

- **Get ahead of invoices instead of reacting after receipt.** Most invoice delays and rejections stem from issues that were knowable before the vendor ever submitted.
- Maintain a rolling anticipated invoice tracker so billing events do not sneak up on the team.
- If a milestone is close but not yet formally accepted, do not assume the vendor will wait -- communicate the status clearly.
- Sharing the CLIN matrix with the vendor early prevents the most common mapping errors.
- Document any billing cadence agreements (monthly, upon delivery, etc.) so there is no ambiguity about when invoices should arrive.
