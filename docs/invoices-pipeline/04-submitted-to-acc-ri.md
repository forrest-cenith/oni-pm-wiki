# Submitted to ACC-RI

## Overview

ONI's invoice package has been sent to ACC-RI for processing. This stage covers submission confirmation, tracking, and responsiveness to any ACC-RI questions or requests for additional information.

## Key Tasks / Checklist

- [ ] Confirm the submission date and method of delivery
- [ ] Save a complete copy of the submitted package (invoice, backup, attachments) exactly as sent
- [ ] Track acknowledgement or receipt confirmation from ACC-RI
- [ ] Monitor for ACC-RI questions, rejections, or requests for clarification
- [ ] Respond to ACC-RI inquiries promptly with accurate supporting information
- [ ] Update the invoice tracker with submission status

## Documents Needed or Produced

| Document | Direction | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Submitted ONI Invoice | Output | The final invoice as sent to ACC-RI |
| Backup Package | Output | All supporting documentation submitted alongside the invoice |
| Submission Email / Confirmation | Output | Record of when and how the invoice was delivered to ACC-RI |

## Common Hangups & Resolutions

| Hangup | Resolution |
|---|---|
| Missing backup documentation flagged by ACC-RI | Provide the requested documents promptly; if they were included in the original submission, resend with a clear reference to where they appear in the package |
| Contract number or CLIN mismatch identified by ACC-RI | Verify internally, correct the invoice if needed, and resubmit with an explanation of the correction |
| ACC-RI questions on support documentation or timing | Respond with specifics -- reference the contract, mod, and deliverable acceptance records that substantiate the charges |
| No acknowledgement from ACC-RI after submission | Follow up within a few business days to confirm receipt and ask for a processing timeline |

## Stakeholders & Roles

| Stakeholder | Role |
|---|---|
| ONI Finance / PM | Submits the invoice package, tracks status, responds to ACC-RI inquiries |
| ACC-RI Billing Staff | Receives, reviews, and processes the invoice for payment |

## Typical Timeline

Submission should occur immediately after ONI review clearance. ACC-RI processing timelines vary, but ONI should track status and follow up if acknowledgement is not received within a few business days.

## Tips & Lessons Learned

- **Save exactly what was submitted, not just the final invoice PDF.** If ACC-RI comes back with questions weeks later, you need the full package as it was sent -- including attachments, cover emails, and backup.
- Maintain a submission log with date, method, recipient, and package contents for every invoice sent to ACC-RI.
- If ACC-RI has a preferred format or submission method, confirm it once and follow it consistently to avoid unnecessary rejections.
- Do not assume submission equals processing -- track until you have confirmation that ACC-RI has acknowledged receipt.
- Build a relationship with your ACC-RI billing POC so questions get resolved quickly rather than through formal channels.
