# Vendor Invoice Received

## Overview

The vendor submits an invoice to ONI. This stage covers intake, initial validation, and routing for internal review. The goal is to confirm the invoice is complete, accurate, and ready for ONI to process against the contract and CLIN structure.

## Key Tasks / Checklist

- [ ] Log the date the invoice was received
- [ ] Check invoice against the contract, agreement, and CLIN structure
- [ ] Verify milestone status and confirm the billed work is accepted or in an acceptable state
- [ ] Validate math -- line items, totals, and any applied rates
- [ ] Confirm supporting documentation is attached and sufficient
- [ ] Confirm no unapproved or out-of-scope work is billed
- [ ] Check that the invoice references the correct contract number, mod version, and billing entity
- [ ] Route the invoice for ONI internal review

## Documents Needed or Produced

| Document | Direction | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Vendor Invoice | Input | The invoice submitted by the vendor to ONI |
| Backup / Supporting Documentation | Input | Labor reports, deliverable evidence, travel receipts, or other substantiation |
| Deliverable Acceptance Records | Input | Proof that billed deliverables or milestones have been accepted |
| Invoice Intake Log | Output | Internal record of receipt date, initial check results, and routing status |

## Common Hangups & Resolutions

| Hangup | Resolution |
|---|---|
| Insufficient backup or missing supporting documentation | Send a specific request to the vendor listing exactly what is missing and the format required |
| Wrong billing entity or invoice formatting errors | Provide the vendor with ONI's required invoice format and entity details; request a corrected resubmission |
| Amounts do not map to CLINs or exceed expected values | Compare line-by-line against the CLIN matrix and flag specific discrepancies back to the vendor |
| Invoice references an outdated mod version or contract number | Confirm the current mod version internally and notify the vendor of the correct reference before they resubmit |

## Stakeholders & Roles

| Stakeholder | Role |
|---|---|
| Vendor | Submits invoice and supporting documentation to ONI |
| ONI PM | Reviews invoice against scope, milestones, and deliverable status |
| ONI Invoicing / Finance | Logs receipt, performs initial validation, routes for review |

## Typical Timeline

Acknowledge receipt the same or next business day. Initial validation and routing should be completed within one to two business days of receipt.

## Tips & Lessons Learned

- **Maintain a standard intake checklist for every invoice.** A consistent checklist prevents items from being missed and speeds up review.
- Do not let invoices sit unacknowledged -- even if review will take time, confirm receipt promptly.
- If the same vendor repeatedly submits incomplete invoices, address the pattern directly rather than correcting each one individually.
- Keep a log of every invoice received with date, amount, and initial check status so nothing falls through the cracks.
- Flag invoices that arrive unexpectedly (not on the anticipated tracker) for closer review.
