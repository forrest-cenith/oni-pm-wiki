# Archive

## Overview
The challenge/project is administratively closed and records are retained for future reference, auditability, and reuse.

## Key Tasks / Checklist
- [ ] Confirm all deliverables received and accepted
- [ ] Confirm final invoices closed
- [ ] Confirm contract status is complete/closed
- [ ] Archive final challenge text, LOEs, proposals, award docs, mods, invoices, and key email chains
- [ ] Capture lessons learned for future challenge drafting and coordination
- [ ] Update internal status tracker to "Archived"

## Documents Needed or Produced
| Document | Purpose | Owner |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Closeout Checklist | Verify all items are complete | ONI PM |
| Lessons Learned Summary | Capture knowledge for future projects | ONI PM |
| Archived Project Folder / Index | Organized record of all project artifacts | ONI PM |

## Email Templates

### Project Closeout
**Subject:** Project Closeout Complete – [Project Name]
**To:** Vendor, government sponsor, internal ONI stakeholders, ACC-RI as needed

**Body:**
```
[Project name] is now administratively closed.

Closeout summary:
- All deliverables: Received and accepted
- Final invoice status: Closed
- Contract status: Complete

All project records have been archived in [location/system].

Thank you to all stakeholders for your contributions to this effort.
```

## Common Hangups & Resolutions

| Hangup | Resolution |
|--------|------------|
| Missing final deliverables | Follow up with vendor before closing |
| Final invoice not matched to CLINs | Reconcile before accepting closeout |
| Modifications still open | Close or cancel open mods before archiving |
| Scattered records across email chains and shared drives | Consolidate into a single project folder |

## Stakeholders & Roles
| Stakeholder | Role |
|-------------|------|
| ONI PM | Manages closeout process |
| Vendor | Provides final deliverables and invoices |
| Government Sponsor | Confirms acceptance |
| ACC-RI Finance / Contracts | Confirms contract closure |

## Typical Timeline
- Should begin immediately after final deliverable acceptance and final invoice closure
- Target: Complete within 1-2 weeks of final delivery

## Tips & Lessons Learned
- Archive the "why" along with the "what": why the challenge was drafted a certain way, why the vendor was selected, why the SOW changed, why invoices were approved
- Future PMs will thank you for clean, organized records
- Lessons learned are most valuable when captured while the project is still fresh
