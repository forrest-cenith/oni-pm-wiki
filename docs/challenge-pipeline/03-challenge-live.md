# Challenge Live

## Overview
The challenge is posted on the marketplace and becomes discoverable to industry. Industry account holders can access the challenge and download associated LOEs.

## Common Hangups & Resolutions

| Hangup | Resolution |
|--------|------------|
| Late government changes after posting | Issue controlled amendment/clarification rather than off-platform verbal guidance |
| Industry asks highly detailed requirement questions | Answer at the need/intent level unless the government wants to narrow scope |
| LOEs and challenge language are inconsistent | Fix immediately — inconsistency creates bad proposals and downstream SOW mismatch |
| Submission standards unclear to vendors | Restate template expectations but remember ONI passes along submissions regardless of formatting compliance |

| Stakeholder | Role |
|-------------|------|

## Typical Timeline
- **Response window:** Typically 30-45 days from posting to down-select
- Template anticipates rapid down-select within this window

## Tips & Lessons Learned
- The more precise the LOEs, the more likely ONI will later need to ask vendors for proposal updates to match government scope
- A broad challenge generates more options; specificity can be layered in later
- Keep a clean log of every clarification issued — these become part of the record
