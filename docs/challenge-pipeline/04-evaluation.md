# Evaluation

## Overview
ONI pre-fills the Technical Evaluation and Award Selection Recommendation document with vendor names and submission dates from the marketplace, then sends it to the Gov Customer to score submissions, document their rationale, and make a selection decision.

## Action Items
- Pre-fill Technical Evaluation document with vendor names and submission dates
- Send closeout package to Gov Customer for evaluation
- Receive completed evaluation and selection from Gov Customer
- Announce selection to ACC-RI

## Exit Criteria
- Gov Customer has completed the Technical Evaluation and Award Selection Recommendation
- Gov Customer has made their selection
- ACC-RI notified of selection

## Common Hangups & Resolutions

| Hangup | Resolution |
|--------|------------|
| Government likes a vendor but proposal doesn't align with LOEs | Select conditionally and request an updated proposal/SOW |
| Review takes longer than posting timeline implied | Send frequent status nudges and set a clear decision deadline |
| Government wants more than one vendor / severable efforts | Map vendors to separate LOEs if the work is severable |

## Stakeholders & Roles
| Stakeholder | Role |
|-------------|------|
| <span class="badge badge-gov">Gov Customer</span> | Evaluates and selects |
| <span class="badge badge-oni">ONI</span> | Facilitates review process |
| <span class="badge badge-vendor">Vendor</span> | May be asked for post-selection updates |

## Typical Timeline
- **Target:** 30-45 days from challenge posting to down-select
- Evaluation itself should be a subset of this window

## Tips & Lessons Learned
- Selection is not the end of scoping — frequently it triggers another documentation cycle
- Preserve written traceability of why a vendor was chosen
- If multiple vendors are strong, consider mapping them to separate severable LOEs
