# Pipeline & Status Definitions

Canonical status definitions for all Copper pipelines. Use these consistently across all tracking and communication.

## Challenge Pipeline Statuses

| Status | Definition | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria |
|--------|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **OTA Intro Call** | Initial engagement with government customer | Government has expressed a need | Intro call completed, inputs requested |
| **Drafting Challenge** | Challenge being written from government inputs | Source material received from government | Draft approved by government |
| **Challenge Live** | Challenge posted and open for industry submissions | Final text approved and uploaded | Response window closed |
| **Evaluation** | Government SMEs reviewing submissions | Submissions collected and packaged for review | Selection decision made |
| **Vendor Coordination** | Selected vendor aligning documents for award | Vendor notified of selection | Clean package submitted to ACC-RI |
| **Prototype Execution** | Prototype work underway under executed agreement | Agreement fully executed | Prototype phase complete |
| **Production Execution** | Follow-on production or transition phase | Transition decision made | Production complete or closed |
| **Archive** | Project administratively closed | All deliverables, invoices, and contracts closed | Records archived and lessons captured |

## Contract Pipeline Statuses

| Status | Definition | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria |
|--------|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **SOW being finalized** | Vendor proposal/SOW being aligned to government scope | Vendor selected, revision cycle active | SOW acceptable to all parties |
| **Project agreement being finalized** | Contracting package assembled and routed for execution | Clean SOW + complete admin package | All signatures obtained |
| **Fully Executed** | All approvals/signatures complete, work authorized | Signatures complete | Kickoff initiated |
| **Modification being worked** | Change to scope, funding, schedule, or admin terms | Change requested and documented | Modification executed or cancelled |
| **Executing** | Contract active, work underway | Agreement executed, kickoff complete | Work complete or contract closed |

## Invoice Pipeline Statuses

| Status | Definition | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria |
|--------|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Invoice Anticipated** | Billing event approaching based on schedule | Milestone approaching or periodic billing due | Vendor invoice received |
| **Vendor Invoice Received** | Invoice received from vendor | Invoice logged and date recorded | Passed to ONI review |
| **ONI Invoice Review** | ONI validating vendor invoice and generating ACC-RI invoice | Vendor invoice received and logged | ONI invoice generated and approved |
| **Submitted to ACC-RI** | ONI invoice package sent to ACC-RI for processing | ONI review complete | ACC-RI confirms processing |
| **Closed** | Invoice fully processed, no further action | ACC-RI confirms closure | Tracker updated, reconciled |

## Dollar Value Tracking (Contracts & Invoices)

For pipelines that track dollar values:
- **Contracts:** Track total contract value and any modification amounts
- **Invoices:** Track individual invoice amounts by CLIN

## Important: Status Language Precision

Always use precise language when communicating status:

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Selected** | Vendor chosen from challenge submissions — NOT yet awarded |
| **Awarded** | Contract package approved and signed — NOT yet fully executed |
| **Fully Executed** | All signatures complete — work can begin |
| **Executing** | Work is actively underway |
| **Closed** | All work, deliverables, and billing complete |

> **Never** say "awarded" when you mean "selected." Never say "executing" before the agreement is fully executed. Precision prevents confusion across all stakeholders.
